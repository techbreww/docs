Integrate BrewBot with your Elgato Stream Deck to trigger bot actions with physical buttons. The integration uses OAuth to connect your Stream Deck to the dashboard.

## Set up the integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Stream Deck integration">
    Open the [BrewBot Dashboard](https://brewbot.techbrew.dev) and go to **Integrations** > **Stream Deck**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the connection">
    Click **Connect Stream Deck** and authorize the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add BrewBot actions">
    In the Stream Deck app, add BrewBot actions to your buttons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your buttons">
    Configure each button with the action you want to trigger.
  </Step>
</Steps>
