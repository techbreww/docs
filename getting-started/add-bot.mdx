<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to the dashboard">
    Go to the [BrewBot Dashboard](https://brewbot.techbrew.dev) and sign in with Discord.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your server">
    Click **Add server** and select the server you want to manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize permissions">
    Authorize the requested permissions — BrewBot needs these to manage roles, channels, and messages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your server">
    Once authorized, your server appears in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need the **Manage Server** permission in Discord to add BrewBot.
</Note>
