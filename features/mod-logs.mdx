Mod logs record server events and moderation actions in a channel you designate. This gives your moderation team a clear audit trail and helps maintain transparency.

## Configure mod logs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Go to the [BrewBot Dashboard](https://brewbot.techbrew.dev) and select your server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mod Logs">
    Open the **Mod Logs** page from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a log channel">
    Choose the channel where the bot should post mod log entries. A private channel visible only to moderators works best.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose events to log">
    Select which events the bot should track. Available events include:

    - **Message deletes** — logs deleted messages with their content
    - **Message edits** — logs the before and after of edited messages
    - **Member bans and unbans** — logs when members are banned or unbanned
    - **Member kicks** — logs when members are kicked
    - **Role changes** — logs when a member's roles are added or removed
    - **Channel updates** — logs channel creation, deletion, and permission changes
    - **Nickname changes** — logs when members change their display names
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Enable only the events you care about. Logging too many events can make the log channel noisy and harder to review.
</Tip>
