The channel and role manager lets you manage your server's channels and roles without opening Discord. Create, edit, reorder, and delete channels and roles directly from the [BrewBot Dashboard](https://brewbot.techbrew.dev).

## Required permissions

The bot needs the following Discord permissions to manage channels and roles:

- **Manage Channels** -- create, edit, reorder, and delete channels
- **Manage Roles** -- create, edit, reorder, and delete roles

<Warning>
Without these permissions, the bot cannot make changes to your server's channels or roles. Make sure the bot's role is positioned above the roles it needs to manage.
</Warning>

## Manage channels

Go to the [BrewBot Dashboard](https://brewbot.techbrew.dev), select your server, and open the **Channel and role manager** page. From here you can:

- Create new text, voice, or category channels
- Edit existing channel names and settings
- Reorder channels by dragging them
- Delete channels you no longer need

## Manage roles

From the same page, switch to the **Roles** tab to:

- Create new roles with custom names and colors
- Edit existing role permissions and settings
- Reorder roles in the hierarchy
- Delete roles you no longer need
