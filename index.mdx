## Free vs premium

BrewBot offers a set of free features and additional premium features.

**Free features:** reaction roles, welcome messages, mod logs, mod agreements.

**Premium features:** bot personalizer, custom commands, social alerts, leveling, live monitor, live tools, birthdays, transcripts, reports, channel/role manager, polls.

See the [pricing and billing](/getting-started/billing) page for plan details.
