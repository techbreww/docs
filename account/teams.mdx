Share dashboard access with your team through Clerk-powered organizations. There are two roles:

- **Admin** — Full access to all server settings, billing, and team management.
- **Member** — Can view and configure features but cannot manage billing or team settings.

## Manage your team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Open the [dashboard](https://brewbot.techbrew.dev) and click your profile icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to team settings">
    Select **Team settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite members">
    Invite members by email or share an invite link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign roles">
    Assign roles to control access levels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each team member must have a Discord account to sign in.
</Note>
